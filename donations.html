A Divine Mandate.
A Campus Transformed.
For with You is the fountain of life; in Your light we see light.

Support Mission

LUCF began in the stillness of early morning prayer—a vision from God to “feed them” and a calling to bring His light to the University of Port Harcourt. What started as a prayer forum beneath the open sky has become a sanctuary of revival and transformation for students every Thursday at 5PM.

We are more than a fellowship. We are a movement of students pursuing God through:

  • Word Teaching
  • Prayer
  • Absolute dependence on the Holy Spirit
  • Miracles

And now, we invite you to be part of this calling.

Why Give?

  • * Host outreach events that draw students to Christ

  • * Share personal stories that demonstrate the power of the Gospel
