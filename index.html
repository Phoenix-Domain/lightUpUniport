About Us

Light Up Uniport Campus Fellowship is a vibrant & faithful company of believers whose love is to see Jesus glorified in the lives of students, lecturers & others over the fire over this campus & other campuses.

Our weekly worship service triggers the manifestation of the Spirit and Power which culminates in healings, prophecies, miracles, breakthroughs, restorations, directions and excellence in all aspects of life.

We do this through;

  • Word Teaching
  • Prayer
  • Absolute dependence on the Holy Spirit
  • Miracles

Students & members are imparted with the spirit of wisdom & excellence to impact their world academically, socially, business & career wise in order to adequately push the frontiers of God's kingdom to overwhelm the kingdom of darkness.

Join us as we take a deep dive into learning the principles of true success in this trying times and for the future.

How we Began

Light Up Uniport Campus Fellowship (LUCF) was born out of a deep passion for consistent prayer, study, and fellowship. On December 18, 2023, during prayer, Emmanuel Orupabo received a vision of multitudes in a university setting, accompanied by the words "FEED THEM." The following night, he heard "UNIPORT", confirming God's call to impact students for His kingdom.

Understanding that the gospel is the foundation but personal testimony is the key to drawing students, he sought a name for the ministry. Scriptures led him to "Light," inspiring LIGHT UP UNIPORT CAMPUS FELLOWSHIP.

LUCF began as a prayer forum on March 21, 2023, meeting Thursdays from 5-6 PM at the university basketball court. On October 17, 2024, the fellowship held its first official gathering in the Abuja Campus, offering an unforgettable experience through deep worship, high praise, transformative word, and miracles.

Emmanuel Orupabo leads LUCF, supported by his wife Ibiso Harry Emmanuel, and they are blessed with two daughters, Emmanuella & Daniella.

This ministry continues to shine as a beacon of spiritual transformation for Uniport students!
